# Sample Co-Founder & Shareholders' Agreement (Synthetic — for demo purposes only)

**Parties:** Alpha Ventures FZE ("Company"), Jane Doe ("Founder A"), John Smith ("Founder B")

## Clause 4 — Indemnity
Each Founder shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company against any and all losses,
damages, claims, and expenses arising from that Founder's breach of this Agreement,
without limitation as to amount or type of loss, including consequential and indirect
losses.

## Clause 7 — Limitation of Liability
Nothing in this Agreement limits either party's liability.

## Clause 9 — Termination
The Company may terminate a Founder's role and equity vesting immediately upon written
notice, at the Company's sole discretion, without cause.

## Clause 11 — Confidentiality
Each party shall keep confidential information secret for a period of 2 years following
termination of this Agreement.

## Clause 13 — Assignment
Either party may freely assign its rights and obligations under this Agreement to any
third party without the consent of the other party.

## Clause 15 — Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of a jurisdiction to be agreed, with disputes
resolved by arbitration at a seat to be determined by the Company.

## Clause 17 — Payment / Buy-Back
Upon a Founder's departure, the Company may repurchase that Founder's shares at a price
determined solely by the Company's board.
